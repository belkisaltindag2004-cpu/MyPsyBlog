About Me

Hello! I am a psychology student and I created this blog with a passion for exploring the complex world of the human mind.

My goal is to take the theories and concepts I've learned during my university education and explain how we can apply them in daily life, rather than leaving them in an academic framework.

Psychology is a branch of science that directly affects all of our lives. On this blog, I aim to help you get to know yourself better and improve your mental health by making scientific information understandable and applicable.
